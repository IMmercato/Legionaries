Consultations

Whispers from the Veterans

Do I need to know Latin or History?

Gods, no. We just need you to solve problems. An aqueduct is just a pipeline that uses gravity—build your modern version and you have our respect.

Can I work in a team?

Yes, squads of up to 3. Gladiators are split equally, with bonus units reinforcing your shared territory pool. Strength is in numbers.

What tech stack is permitted?

Anything. Web, hardware, physical builds, or sims. As long as it's functional, documented, and moves the empire forward.

How are Gladiators scored?

Consuls evaluate: functionality (does it work?), depth (was it hard?), connection to antiquity, and the clarity of your scrolls (docs).

What happens if territories clash?

History happens. The largest army takes the land, but no one loses their men. Those pushed back simply find a new front to conquer.

Is there a limit on submissions?

No hard limit, but the Senate values quality. One masterwork is worth more than five half-hearted attempts. Don't farm shallow glory.

How long does the campaign run?

Through the heat of Summer 2026. You can submit throughout the campaign as the map evolves in real-time.

Is my idea too "weird"?

Weird ideas won wars. If a Centurion could understand the problem you're solving, then march forward without fear. Weird is welcome.
